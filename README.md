# AdventureWorks Customer & Contact Channel Analysis

**Power BI Dashboard** analyzing customer demographics, contact channels, email promotions, and geographic distribution.

## Overview
In-depth exploration of ~20K persons in the AdventureWorks database, focusing on engagement, segmentation, and reach to support targeted marketing and CRM strategies.

## Key Metrics
- **Total Persons**: 19.97K | **Countries**: 12
- **Email/Phone Coverage**: 100%
- **Customer Split**: 93% Individual (18.5K) | 7% Business (1.3K)
- **Avg Orders per Customer**: 1.59 overall (Business: 2.85 | Individual: 1.50)
- **Email Opt-In**: 56% No Promotions | 25% AW Only | 19% Partners+AW

## Dashboard Pages
1. **Engagement Overview** – High-level KPIs, person types, promotion opt-ins, phone distribution
2. **Email Promotion Behaviour** – Opt-in impact on orders, breakdown by person type
3. **Geographic Reach** – Country & state/province analysis with drill-down hierarchy
4. **Customer Segmentation** – Individual vs Business, address types
5. **Contact Channel Deep Dive** – Phone counts, promo matrix, channel preferences

## Technologies
- Power BI Desktop
- DAX measures (averages, percentages, hierarchies)
- Visuals: Donut charts, bar charts, treemaps, matrices, geographic breakdowns

## Insights Delivered
- Business customers are significantly more active despite being a small segment
- Near-universal contact coverage enables broad marketing campaigns
- Clear geographic hotspots for regional targeting (e.g., California, Washington)
- Promotion levels show minimal difference in average order frequency
